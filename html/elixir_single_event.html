SIB course: "Protein Bioinformatics: Sequence-Structure-Function"

Mon 25-01-2016 (All day) to Thu 28-01-2016 (All day)
Basel, Switzerland
Workshops and courses

This course, held in Basel from 25-28 January 2016, is addressed to biologists who work with proteins in any context. The SIB developers behind the tools for curation, integration and inference of protein structural and functional data will provide lectures and practicals, culminating in a "bring your own protein(s)" workshop.

Further information and registration form are available here: http://edu.isb-sib.ch/course/view.php?id=240

More SIB courses are available at: http://www.isb-sib.ch/training.html
